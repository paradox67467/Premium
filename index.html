Our Story

Born from
Passion,
Built on
Artistry

In a sunlit atelier in Paris's 7th arrondissement, Maison Aura was born — not from commerce, but from a deep-rooted belief that clothing is the most intimate art form humans create.

Every pattern is drawn by hand. Every fabric chosen with obsessive care. We exist at the precise intersection of tradition and tomorrow.

Bespoke Service

Your vision,
tailored
to perfection

Book a private consultation with our master tailors at any of our four ateliers worldwide. Every appointment includes champagne, patience, and perfection.

I.

Provenance of Materials

We trace every thread to its origin. Our silks come from a single family-owned farm in Como. Our cashmere from the high plateaus of Inner Mongolia. Excellence begins at the source.

II.

The Art of the Seam

Each garment passes through twelve hands before reaching yours. Every seam is finished by needle, not machine. Our tailors train for seven years before touching a Maison Aura piece.

III.

Sustainable Legacy

We design with tomorrow in mind. Zero-waste pattern cutting, natural dyes, and a lifetime repair program ensure your piece endures — and evolves alongside you.
